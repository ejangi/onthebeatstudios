Friday, October 12th 2012

Fresh and exciting news for us at the studio, we have just added a Lynx Aroura 8 AD/DA convertor. These are used in most professional studios worldwide.

Also, the centre piece of our studio is now a Soundcraft sapphyre console, which when new cost around 30k. While it sounds great and is in full working order we will be slowly modding it between projects just so it sounds even better.

Saturday, June 16th 2012

Hey all, we have been busy the last few months doing a few vocal tracks for 2 different hip-hop artist & also some opera tracks as well.

We provided live sound for the Livingstone Christian College primary musical and a few events at Groves Christian College too.

In the next few months we will be doing a single for hardcore band Chasing Indigo (which is slated for mastering by Matt Grey Mastering) and rock band Hugoslide will be in to track a single.

Monday, February 27th 2012

Local Redlands Bay band The Righteous & The Wicked have just done a demo single and you can hear it here.

We are also about to start a hardcore E.P. for local band Chasing Indigo which will then be sent to Matt Grey Mastering for final touches.

Don't forget to check out our studio facebook page!

Wednesday, September 14th 2011

The Studio is now a proud owner of a Kawai upright piano! This beautiful new addition will live up at the main house and is available for your next recording.

We have also added a Sennheiser E614 to the mic collection, a new power amp for the Faith Dynaudios and an Alesis RA150 that replaces our ever dying Samson Amp.

All these upgrades mean that we're primed and ready for another great season of recording.

Monday, August 1st 2011

We have just finished mastering a full length album for CJ'S Night & Day which we also recorded as well.

We are also starting a EP for local punk band Chasing Indigo in the next few weeks. Also we are doing a live recording for the brisbane male voice choir in september as well mixing there FOH for there show too.

Sunday, February 6th 2011

Jazz quartet CJ's Night & Day are hitting the studio again in February to record there next full length album. And Emma Nainby is set to record 3 new songs in the next few months. Stay tuned.
